Caesar Salad Recipe

Description

This Caesar salad recipe is a classic and refreshing dish, perfect as a starter or a light meal. Crisp romaine lettuce is tossed with a creamy Caesar dressing, topped with croutons and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • Optional: grilled chicken, bacon, or anchovies

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and Caesar dressing. Toss until the lettuce is evenly coated.
  2. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and croutons. Toss gently to combine.
  3. Optional: Top with grilled chicken, bacon, or anchovies for added flavor.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
